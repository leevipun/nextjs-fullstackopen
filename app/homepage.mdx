# Blog App

Welcome to **Blog App**, a full-stack web application for reading and sharing blog posts.

## Features

- **Browse blogs** — Discover posts from the community
- **Create posts** — Share your thoughts with others
- **Reading list** — Save blogs to read later and track what you have read
- **Like posts** — Show appreciation for great content
- **User profiles** — See what others are writing

## Get started

Head over to the [blog posts](/blogs) page to explore posts, or [sign up](/register) to create an account and start writing.

---

Built with Next.js, Drizzle ORM, and Neon Postgres.
